Welcome to La Salle ACM

We represent La Salle's chapter of the ACM organization. Our goals as a club is to provide those interested in computer science and IT the opportunity to connect with one another and gain opportunities to gain and use skills outside of the classroom.

We have a wide variety of different events throughout the year from hackathons and competitions to interesting talks and career expos. We also will hold a few social events throughout the year. No matter who you are we are a great resource for you if you have an interest in technology.
