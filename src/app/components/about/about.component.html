Welcome to La Salle ACM

We represent La Salle's chapter of the ACM organization. Our goal as a club is to provide those interested in computer science and IT to connect with other students, gain opportunities, and utilize skills outside of the classroom.

We have a wide variety of events throughout the year, from hackathons and competitions to exciting talks and career expos. We also will hold a few social events throughout the year. No matter who you are, we are a great resource if you are interested in technology.
